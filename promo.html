Something stirs beneath the mountain

In the deepest and darkest dungeon workshop, Iron Goblin is busy inventing a next generation cooperative multiplayer roleplaying game.

We focus on building games for friends. Games inspired by the camaraderie felt when playing tabletop roleplaying games like D&D. Our goal is to reward players with immersive and engaging worlds. Places to be expressive in, to face challenges together, to create new memories, to strengthen bonds.
