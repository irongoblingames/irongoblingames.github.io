In the deepest and darkest dungeon workshop, Iron Goblin is busy inventing the next generation multiplayer RPG. Drawing on their decades of hero slaying, trap laying and riddle saying antics to make sure the next band of adventurers meet with an experience for which they are not prepared.

Founded by game industry veteran Dean Roskell, whos world-building journey started out crafting text-based dungeons on the Commodore Vic-20 before levelling up to shape the lands of Thedas in Dragon Age, and then jetting off into the stars to help form the worlds of Mass Effect.
